About

Tom’s Toys is a locally owned, family operated toy store located in Beverly Hills, California. They have been in business since 1998. Tom’s Toy Store has a wide variety of toys, board games, collectables, and other fun things and it is for all ages! There are three locations: San Luis Osibo, Montrose, and Beverly Hills.
